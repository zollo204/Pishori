Baked Turmeric Chicken and Rice

1 cup whole milk yogurt, Kosher salt, 2 tsp Kashmiri chili powder, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 2...

Read More...

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup whole milk yogurt
  • Kosher salt to taste
  • 2 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 4 boneless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix yogurt, salt, chili powder, and turmeric. Marinate chicken for 1 hour.
  3. Sauté onions and garlic until soft.
  4. Add rice and broth, simmer briefly.
  5. Place chicken on rice in a baking dish, cover with foil.
  6. Bake 40 minutes covered, then 10 minutes uncovered.
  7. Garnish with cilantro before serving.

Vegetable Rice

3 cups water, 1 ½ cups brown rice, 2 tbsp peanut oil, 1 small yellow onion, 1 small...

Read More...

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups water
  • 1½ cups quick-cooking brown rice
  • 2 tbsp peanut oil
  • 1 small yellow onion, chopped
  • 1 small carrot, diced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, corn, bell pepper)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pot and sauté onion and carrot until soft.
  2. Add mixed vegetables and cook for 3 minutes.
  3. Pour in water, bring to a boil.
  4. Add rice, reduce heat, cover, and simmer 20 minutes or until water is absorbed.
  5. Fluff rice with a fork and season with salt and pepper.

Chicken Pilau

1/2 chicken with bone, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste, 1 tsp cumin seeds, 1/2 tsp...

Read More...

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 chicken with bone, cut into pieces
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 3 cups chicken stock
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Oil for cooking
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil and fry onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste and spices; cook 2 minutes.
  3. Add chicken pieces, brown evenly.
  4. Pour chicken stock, bring to boil.
  5. Add washed rice, stir gently, cover, and simmer on low heat until cooked.
  6. Fluff with fork and serve hot.

Mushroom Rice

2 Tbsp butter, 1 Tbsp coconut oil, 2 large onions finely sliced...

Read More...

Ingredients:

  • 2 Tbsp butter or margarine
  • 1 Tbsp coconut oil
  • 2 large onions, finely sliced
  • 300g mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat butter and coconut oil; sauté onions until caramelized.
  2. Add mushrooms, cook until tender.
  3. Add rice, stir for 2 minutes.
  4. Pour in vegetable broth, season, and simmer covered until rice is cooked.

Fried Rice

3 cups water, 1 ½ cups brown rice, 2 tbsp peanut oil, 1 chopped yellow onion...

Read More...

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups water
  • 1½ cups quick-cooking brown rice
  • 2 tbsp peanut oil
  • 1 small yellow onion, chopped
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • Soy sauce and salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook rice in water, drain and cool.
  2. Heat oil, sauté onion and vegetables.
  3. Push veggies aside, scramble eggs in pan.
  4. Add rice, soy sauce, and mix well; cook for 5 minutes.

Coconut Rice

2 cups jasmine rice, 2 cups coconut milk, 1 3/4 cups water, 2 tbsp desiccated coconut...

Read More...

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Thai jasmine rice
  • 2 cups Kentaste coconut milk
  • 1 3/4 cups water
  • 2 tbsp desiccated coconut
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Rinse rice until water runs clear.
  2. Heat oil, add mustard seeds until they pop.
  3. Add rice, coconut milk, water, and salt.
  4. Cook covered on low heat until liquid is absorbed.
  5. Sprinkle desiccated coconut and fluff before serving.

Rice Beans

250 g pishori rice, pre-soaked red beans, Pilipili hoho, 3 carrots, 1 bunch Dania...

Read More...

Ingredients:

  • 250 g pishori rice
  • Pre-soaked red beans
  • 1 Pilipili hoho (green chili), chopped
  • 3 carrots, chopped
  • 1 bunch fresh coriander (Dania)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups water or broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onions and carrots until soft.
  2. Add beans, water, and cook until beans are tender.
  3. Add rice, green chili, and season.
  4. Cook on low heat until rice is done.
  5. Mix in fresh coriander before serving.

Chicken Biriani

500–600 g chicken, 1 tsp garlic ginger paste, biriani spices, cooking oil...

Read More...

Ingredients:

  • 500–600 g chicken pieces
  • 1 tsp garlic ginger paste
  • 2 tbsp biriani spice mix
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and fried onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken with garlic ginger paste and biriani spices for 30 minutes.
  2. Fry onions until golden, set aside some for garnish.
  3. Cook chicken in oil until browned.
  4. Add partially cooked rice over chicken, cover and cook on low heat until done.
  5. Garnish with fried onions and fresh coriander before serving.
